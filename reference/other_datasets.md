# Other Immigration Datasets for Reporters

The DDP arrests and detainers data answers *who ICE arrested and where*. These datasets fill in the rest of the picture.

---

## Who Is Being Arrested and Detained

| Dataset | Where to find it | What it adds |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| **DDP Detentions** | deportationdata.org/data/ice.html (same FOIA release) | Where people go after arrest; how long they're held; bond amounts; release reasons |
| **DDP Encounters** | deportationdata.org (same release) | Pre-arrest contact events — shows enforcement activity before a formal arrest |
| **ICE Biweekly Detention Stats** | ice.gov/detain/detention-management | Current detained population; facility-level counts; credible fear, bond, segregation — updated every two weeks |
| **ICE Deaths in Detention** | ice.gov (search "detainee death reporting") | Every reported death in ICE custody since FY2018 |

---

## Local Agency Cooperation

| Dataset | Where to find it | What it adds |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| **287(g) Participating Agencies** | ice.gov/identify-and-arrest/287g | Official list of agencies with active ICE cooperation agreements; updated when new agreements are signed |
| **SCAAP Awards** | bja.ojp.gov/program/scaap | Federal reimbursements to counties and states for holding people ICE flags; shows which jails profit from cooperation |
| **Vera Institute ICE Facilities** | github.com/vera-institute/ice-detention-trends | Geocoded facility list with AOR assignments, capacity, and inspection data |

---

## Immigration Courts

| Dataset | Where to find it | What it adds |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| **TRAC Immigration** | trac.syr.edu | The most journalist-friendly immigration court database — searchable by judge, court, nationality, outcome; updated monthly |
| **EOIR Case Data** | justice.gov/eoir/foia-library-0 | Raw immigration court records from the 1990s to present; comprehensive but requires processing |

---

## Contracts and Spending

| Dataset | Where to find it | What it adds |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| **USASpending.gov** | usaspending.gov | Federal contracts for detention facilities, transportation, surveillance; filter by awarding agency = ICE or DHS |
| **SAM.gov / USMS IGA Contracts** | sam.gov | US Marshals intergovernmental agreements with county jails; shows which local jails are paid to hold people |

---

## Policy and Legal

| Dataset | Where to find it | What it adds |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Federal Register** | federalregister.gov | Immigration rulemakings, executive orders, policy notices; searchable by agency |
| **CourtListener / PACER** | courtlistener.com | Federal court filings; search §2241 habeas corpus petitions to find detained people fighting removal orders |
| **LegiScan** | legiscan.com | State legislation on sanctuary policies, 287(g) bans, detainer compliance — all 50 states |

---

## Story Context and Verification

| Dataset | Where to find it | What it adds |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Census Bureau ACS** | data.census.gov | Foreign-born population by county — use as denominator for per-capita rates |
| **CBP Encounter Data** | cbp.gov/newsroom/stats | Border encounters by sector and nationality; complements ICE interior enforcement data |
| **DHS Immigration Enforcement Stats** | dhs.gov/immigration-statistics | Annual summary statistics covering deportations, returns, overstays — slower release cycle |

---

## A Note on Combining These

A complete local immigration story usually draws from multiple columns:

- **The DDP** tells you who was arrested and where.
- **TRAC / EOIR** tells you what happened in court.
- **ICE detention stats** tell you where people are held and for how long.
- **287(g) list + SCAAP** tell you which local governments are cooperating and getting paid for it.
- **CourtListener** tells you who is fighting back legally.

The strongest stories combine at least two of these — a number from the data, and a human story that came from that number.
